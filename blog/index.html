Breezy's Catering est: March 2016

My catering business focuses on bringing college students who live on campus a home cooked meals. All food ingredients will be posted on a flyer in case of food allergies. This will include a choice of one meat, a choice of 2 side items and a choice of 1 dessert. Headquarters will be in East Lansing Michigan, with more business to come, business out the Metro Detroit area.

There are 7 main course meals: Fried Chicken wings Baked Chicken Grilled Chicken (Depending on weather) Breaded fried pork chops Steak Lasagna BBQ or Gravy Meatballs There are 4 side items: Green Beans Mac and Cheese Spaghetti Yellow Rice.
